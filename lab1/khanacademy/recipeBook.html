Pasta sauce

  • Time: 30 mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
dried pasta 400g
double cream 150ml
parmesan 25g

Step 1: Half fill a large saucepan with water, add plenty of salt and bring to the boil.

Step 2: Just before the pasta is ready, pour the double cream into a small saucepan, add the garlic and bring to a very gentle simmer, stirring constantly.
